Our Heritage

A Story Brewed with Love & Tradition

From a humble street cart to Bapunagar's favourite summer destination — our journey is one of passion, family, and unforgettable flavours.

🏪

Humble Beginnings

Megha Sarbat started as a small street cart (lari) with a dream — to bring fresh, cooling drinks to the hardworking people of Bapunagar during scorching Ahmedabad summers.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Business

Run with heart by our family, every Gola and Sarbat is prepared with fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and the warmth only a family business can offer.

❤️

Community Love

For over 25 summers, thousands of families, children and regulars have made Megha Sarbat a cherished part of their Ahmedabad memories.

1999

The Beginning

Megha Sarbat opens its first lari near D-Mart Bapunagar.

2000s

Growing Flavours

Expanding from basic sarbats to signature Baraf Gola with 8+ exciting flavours.

2010s

Community Icon

Became a seasonal landmark — queues forming every summer, word spreading across Ahmedabad.

2020s

Bulk Orders Added

Launched color bottle syrups for bulk order — bringing the Megha Sarbat taste to homes and events.

Today

25+ Years Strong 🎉

Still serving with the same passion, now with a digital presence to reach even more customers.
